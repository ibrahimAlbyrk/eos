---
description: "MCP tool — list_peers"
variables:
  - ASK_PEER_TOOL
---

List the peer workers you can consult — your siblings, spawned to collaborate with you. Returns each peer's id, name, a one-line summary of what it specializes in, and whether it's currently available.

Takes no arguments. Call it before {{ASK_PEER_TOOL}} to find the right peer by specialty, then pass that peer's id to consult it. An empty list means no peers are available right now — proceed on your own and note it in your report.
