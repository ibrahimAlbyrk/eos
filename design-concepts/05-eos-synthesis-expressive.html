▶ How the concepts were merged & matched to the brand

Glance over click from 03

The bar item carries live signal so you rarely need to open it: a tabular mono count (never reflows) plus a breathing activity equalizer in --ok green — each bar an active worker, density and tempo scaling with the fleet size.

The living dawn-star from 02

02's breathing orb, but made of the real logo: the Eos dawn-star is the running identity — it blooms from monochrome into its brand-blue halo and breathes faster the busier it gets. Off-palette dawn hues from 02 were dropped for the real accent.

Right-pinned ticker from 01

01's discipline floor: the item's right edge is pinned and it grows leftward, so neighbours and the popover never shift. One easing drives every morph; the check strokes itself in; idle and running stay strictly monochrome — colour means something.

Sequential completion queue from 03 · 01

The signature interaction: simultaneous finishes queue and play one-by-one — a green check, the name, a hairline draining ~1.3s, then the next slides up. A +N badge counts those still waiting; the bar settles to the live view when empty.

Real liquid glass brand

The popover uses the exact recipe: blur(22) saturate(170%) over rgba(--glass,.5), the 135° rim highlight via mask-composite, and the app's shadow stack. The menu bar mimics OS vibrancy the same way. Real tokens, both themes.

Real colour semantics brand

Accent #6ea4e8 is brand/attention (the star's halo, selection rings) — not running. Running activity and completion are --ok green; failure is --err red. The bar logo stays template-tinted; the popover header shows the full-colour mark.

Type note: the app ships Plus Jakarta Sans + JetBrains Mono; substituted with the system UI / mono stack here to honour the no-network, system-fonts-only constraint.
